FAQ

What is the test program?

+

The pre launch phase. The Joyble Test Program gives early access to our platform before it's publicly available. You'll be among the first to explore local events, host your own, and shape how the platform works through your real-world use

We especially welcome local hosts, venues, and community spaces. By joining early, you can:

- See how Joyble fits into your daily routines

- Reach people nearby who are actively looking to connect and catch up

- Test how the platform supports attendance and visibility

- Track the flow of people in your space — and get smart, simple insights into what's working and when to expect your next crowd

- Engage with hosts, chat, make friends, shuffle the rooms and have fun in the community

- Be one of the first trusted places users engage with

If you have ideas, needs, or requests — don't hesitate to reach out. We're happy to explore tailor-made features or support that fits your space. Feel free to contact us for more information

Do I need to be a professional host or business to use it?

+

No, everyone can interact on the platform

How is Joyble different from existing social media?

+

We are trying to build an event and activity based platform where we encourage hosts, content-creators, venues and anyone who's ever said: "What are we doing tonight?" in a buddies group chat to give it a try. Also, we suggest to anyone who's ever responded: "idk" to do the same. Moreover, it's designed ergonomically in Denmark, our development team is trying to re-shape the idea of social media!

We need platforms to be ergonomic

Not just for our hands - but for our minds, our culture and our communities.

Most platforms aren't built that way. They demand attention, not intention. They call themselves social — but rarely bring us together in real life.

Some people are natural connectors. They host, gather, create moments from nothing. They set the vibe and open the door.

Others are on the lookout. Open to something spontaneous, curious about where the night might lead, hoping for a plan that feels right.

And too often, we just miss each other. Plans stay stuck in the chat. People hesitate. Moments don't come together.

Still, we're all after the same thing: Something easy. Something real. A time and place where it just works — where you feel like you belong.

Joyble isn't a feed. It's not about content. It's a way to match people with the moments that are meant for them.

Whether you're setting the table, or looking for your seat at it — you deserve connection that feels alive, and true.

That's it. Just time well spent, with people who matter.
